# Lyria 3 Pro — AI Music Generator

[Lyria 3 Pro](https://lyria3pro.pro) is an AI-powered music generator built on Google Lyria 3 Pro. Generate full-length songs with vocals, lyrics, and instrumentals — all from a simple text prompt.

**Try it now:** [https://lyria3pro.pro](https://lyria3pro.pro)

## Features

- **Text-to-Music** — Describe the song you want, and AI generates it for you
- **Full-Length Songs** — Generate tracks up to 3 minutes with vocals, lyrics, and instrumentals
- **Multiple AI Models** — Powered by Lyria 3 Pro, Suno V5, V4.5, V4, and more
- **MP3 Download** — Download your generated tracks instantly
- **No Music Skills Required** — Anyone can create professional-sounding music

## How It Works

1. Enter a text prompt describing the music you want
2. Choose an AI model (Lyria 3 Pro, Suno V5, etc.)
3. Click generate and wait for your song
4. Download the MP3 and enjoy
